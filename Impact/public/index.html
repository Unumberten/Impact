What we do

MDIHub is an innovative co-working techno hub in the heart of Mahikeng- capital city of North West Province, with our focus being on unearthing and cultivating innovative solutions that will ultimately assist communities to acclimatize in this vast changing technological world, while creating new business pipelines, new and future jobs, improving efficiency and effectiveness in the North West province

With the world moving towards seamless and integrated solutions, MIHub has created an innovative platform to support entrepreneurs to drive their business with the latest technology.

We will ignite innovative excellence, build products and solutions that will ultimately bear social, technological and economic benefits to the North West Province while competing on a global stage. “innovate for tomorrow’s world today.

  • The hub hosts hackathons, where programmers and others involved in software development collaborate intensively on software projects
  • We offer tech entrepreneurs assistance and training in various aspects of business opperations.
  • Through our incubation program, we support innoivative tech start-ups and SMMEs by giving them resources to develop their products and services.
